<sub> - Subscript

🧠 What it does:

  • Displays text slightly below the normal line.
  • Used for chemical formulas or mathematical expressions.
  • Reduces the font size of the text automatically.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To show subscripts in chemical notations (e.g., H2O).
  • To represent variables in formulas or equations.
  • When you need precise scientific or technical formatting.

💻 Syntax:

<p>Water formula is H<sub>2</sub>O.</p>

🔍 Output:

Water formula is H2O.
