<p> - Paragraph

🧠 What it does:

  • Creates a block of text with vertical spacing.
  • Automatically adds margin before and after the paragraph.
  • Helps organize content into readable chunks.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To write any normal body text in a webpage.
  • When separating ideas, topics, or sections in content.
  • Inside articles, blogs, or documentation-style pages.

💻 Syntax:

<p>This is a Paragraph</p>

🔍 Output:

This is a Paragraph
