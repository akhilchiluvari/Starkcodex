<strong> - Strong Importance

🧠 What it does:

  • Makes the text bold (visually).
  • Adds semantic importance to the content.
  • Improves screen reader emphasis.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To highlight important text.
  • To emphasize something critical or urgent.
  • For better accessibility and semantic HTML.

💻 Syntax:

<p>Please <strong>do not share</strong> your password.</p>

🔍 Output:

Please do not share your password.
