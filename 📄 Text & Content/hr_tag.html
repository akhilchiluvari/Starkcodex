<hr> - Horizontal Rule

🧠 What it does:

  • Creates a thematic break or divider in content.
  • Draws a horizontal line across the page.
  • Used to visually separate sections or ideas.

🕰️ When to use:

  • When you want a visual divider between different topics or thoughts.
  • To indicate a shift in narrative or theme.
  • Between footer and main content for clarity.

💻 Syntax:

<hr>
