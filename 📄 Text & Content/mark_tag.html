<mark> - Mark Text

🧠 What it does:

  • Highlights text with a yellow background by default.
  • Draws attention to specific words or phrases.
  • Inline tag that doesn't break the text flow

🕰️ When to use:

  • To emphasize search results or keywords.
  • To show what a user searched or matched.
  • To highlight important text in notes or docs.

💻 Syntax:

<p>This is a <mark>very important</mark> point.</p>

🔍 Output:

This is a very important point.
