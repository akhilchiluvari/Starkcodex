<br> - Line Break

🧠 What it does:

  • Creates a single line break within inline or block content.
  • Forces text to continue on the next line without a new block.
  • Helps break content visually without separating it semantically.

🕰️ When to use:

  • In poems, addresses, or lyrics where line breaks matter.
  • Inside a single paragraph where you want a manual line shift.
  • When formatting signatures or multi-line contact info.

💻 Syntax:

<p>This is Line One.</p><br><p>This is Line Two.</p>

🔍 Output:

This is Line One.
This is Line Two.
