<em> - Emphasis

🧠 What it does:

  • Emphasizes text semantically.
  • Renders content in *italic* by default.
  • Helps screen readers interpret stressed words.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To show a word is important or emotionally stressed.
  • To indicate contrast (e.g. *this* not *that*)
  • For spoken tone emphasis in assistive tech.

💻 Syntax:

<p>You </em> need to try this!</p>

🔍 Output:

You really need to try this!
