<small> - Side Comment

🧠 What it does:

  • Displays text in a smaller size than normal.
  • Often used for legal disclaimers or side notes.
  • Does not affect meaning or emphasis, just size.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To show copyright or legal disclaimers.
  • To reduce visual importance of extra info.
  • To style footnotes or fine print content.

💻 Syntax:

<p>The prodect is awesome. <small>Terms & conditions apply.</small>

🔍 Output:

The product is awesome. Terms & conditions apply.
