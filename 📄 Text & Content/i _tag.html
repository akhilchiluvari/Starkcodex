<i> - Idiomatic

🧠 What it does:

  • Displays text in *italic style* (no semantics).
  • Used for phrases like foreign words or technical terms.
  • Purely visual, no special meaning for screen readers.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To *style* text without implying emphasis or importance.
  • For book titles, Latin phrases, or variables in code.
  • When the content's meaning doesn't need screen reader stress.

💻 Syntax:

<p>The word <i>café</i> is from French.</p>

🔍 Output:

The word Café is from French.
