<abbr> - Abbreviation

🧠 What it does:

  • Represents an **abbreviation or acronym** like HTML or NASA.
  • Displays a **tooltip** with full form on hover.
  • Helps with **accessibility and SEO** (screen readers know what it means).

🕰️ When to use:

  • When using **short forms** that aren't obvious (e.g., AI, CSS).
  • To make content more **user-friendly and accessible**.
  • For professional docs, academic articles, tech blogs, etc.

💻 Syntax:

<p>I am learning <abbr title="HyperText Markup Language">HTML</abbr>.</p>

🔍 Output:

I am learning HTML.
