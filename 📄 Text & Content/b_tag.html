<b> - Bring Attention To

🧠 What it does:

  • Displays text in bold style.
  • Applies only visual styling — no semantic meaning.
  • Screen readers treat it like normal text.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To visually highlight text without implying importance.
  • For names, labels, or keywords in documents.
  • When semantic emphasis is not required.

💻 Syntax:

<p>Welcome to <b>Stark Industries</b> HQ.</p>

🔍 Output:

Welcome to Stark Industries HQ.
