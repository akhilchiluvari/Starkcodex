<sup> - Superscript

🧠 What it does:

  • Displays text **slightly above the baseline** (like flying text ✈️).
  • Used to show **exponents**, **footnotes**, or **ordinal indicators**.
  • It's a **semantic** tag — tells browsers and screen readers that the text is superscripted.

🕰️ When to use:

  • In **math expressions** like x2.
  • When adding **footnote references** like This is cool1.
  • For **scientific formulas**, like E = mc2

💻 Syntax:

<p>Are of square is side<sup>2</sup>.</p>

🔍 Output:

Area of square is side2.
