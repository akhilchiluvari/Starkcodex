<address> - Contact Information

🧠 What it does:

  • Specifies contact information for its nearest article, section, or the whole page.
  • Can include physical addresses, emails, phone numbers, or social media links.
  • Improves semantic meaning for search engines and assistive technologies.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To display the author’s or organization’s contact details.
  • Inside a footer for website-wide contact info.
  • Inside an article to specify the author’s contact information.

💻 Syntax:

<address>
    Written by <a href="mailto:info@example.com">John Doe</a>.<br>
    Visit us at:<br>
    Example.com<br>
    123 Main Street, Hometown<br>
    Country
</address>

🔍 Output:

Written by John Doe.
Visit us at:
Example.com
123 Main Street, Hometown
Country
