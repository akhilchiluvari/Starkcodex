<article> - Article Section

🧠 What it does:

  • Represents self-contained content that can stand independently.
  • Often used for blog posts, news articles, or forum entries.
  • Can be distributed or reused across different platforms.

🕰️ When to use:

  • When creating standalone, reusable content like blog posts or news updates.
  • For forum posts, product cards, or user-submitted content.
  • Inside a webpage to separate different topics clearly.

💻 Syntax:

<article>
    <h2>Breaking News</h2>
    <p>AI is revolutionizing the tech industry faster than ever.</p>
</article>

🔍 Output:

Breaking News

AI is revolutionizing the tech industry faster than ever.
