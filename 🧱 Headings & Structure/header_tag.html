<header> - Header Section

🧠 What it does:

  • Defines the introductory content for a webpage or a section.
  • Often contains the site logo, title, and navigation links.
  • Helps organize top-of-page content in a semantic way.

🕰️ When to use:

  • At the top of a webpage for branding and navigation.
  • At the start of an article or section to introduce its content.
  • To improve semantic structure and accessibility.

💻 Syntax:

<header>
    <h1>My Website</h1>
    <nav>
        <a href="#home">Home</a>
        <a href="#about">About</a>
        <a href="#contact">Contact</a>
    </nav>
</header>

🔍 Output:

My Website

Home About Contact
