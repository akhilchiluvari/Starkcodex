<main> - Main Content Section

🧠 What it does:

  • Represents the main content area of a webpage.
  • Should contain content unique to that page (not repeated across other pages).
  • Improves semantic structure for accessibility and SEO.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To wrap the primary, unique content of a webpage.
  • When you want assistive technologies to easily locate the main content.
  • To separate central content from headers, footers, and sidebars.

💻 Syntax:

<main>
    <h2>Welcome to My Website</h2>
    <p>This is the main content of the page.</p>
</main>

🔍 Output:

Welcome to My Website

This is the main content of the page.
