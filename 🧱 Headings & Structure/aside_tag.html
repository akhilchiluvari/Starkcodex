<aside> - Sidebar / Secondary Content

🧠 What it does:

  • Represents content indirectly related to the main content.
  • Commonly used for sidebars, tips, ads, or related links.
  • Improves semantic meaning for search engines and screen readers.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To display supplementary information without breaking the main flow.
  • For notes, pull quotes, quick tips, or side navigation.
  • For related articles or external resources.

💻 Syntax:

<article>
    <h2>How to Grow Basil Indoors</h2>
    <p>Basil loves sunlight and warmth...</p>

    <aside>
        <h3>Quick Tip</h3>
        <p>Place your basil near a south-facing window for best results.</p>
    </aside>
</article>

🔍 Output:

How to Grow Basil Indoors

Basil loves sunlight and warmth...

Quick Tip

Place your basil near a south-facing window for best results.
