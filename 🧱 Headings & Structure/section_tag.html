<section> - Content Section

🧠 What it does:

  • Defines a thematic grouping of content within a webpage.
  • Helps organize related content into clear, structured blocks.
  • Improves readability, accessibility, and semantic meaning.

🕰️ When to use:

  • When grouping related content under a single theme or topic.
  • Inside articles, pages, or main content areas for structure.
  • To break content into logical sections for easier navigation.

💻 Syntax:

<section>
    <h2>About Us</h2>
    <p>We are a tech company building modern web solutions.</p>
</section>

🔍 Output:

About Us

We are a tech company building modern web solutions.
