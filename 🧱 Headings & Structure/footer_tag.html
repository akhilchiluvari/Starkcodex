<footer> - Footer Section

🧠 What it does:

  • Defines the closing content for a webpage or section.
  • Usually contains copyright info, contact details, or related links.
  • Helps structure bottom-of-page info in a semantic way.

🕰️ When to use:

  • At the bottom of a webpage for legal or navigation purposes.
  • Inside articles or sections to add source references or related links.
  • To improve semantic structure and accessibility.

💻 Syntax:

<footer>
    <p>&copy; 2025 My Website</p>
    <nav>
        <a href="#privacy">Privacy Policy</a> |
        <a href="#terms">Terms of Service</a>
    </nav>
</footer>

🔍 Output:

© 2025 My Website

Privacy Policy | Terms of Service
