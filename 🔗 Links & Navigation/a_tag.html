<a> - Anchor (Hyperlink)

🧠 What it does:

  • Creates hyperlinks to other pages, sections, files, or resources.
  • Can link to external websites or internal parts of the same page.
  • Supports attributes like href, target, and title.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To navigate users to different pages or websites.
  • For linking to downloadable files or documents.
  • To jump to specific sections using anchors.

💻 Syntax:

<a href="https://example.com" target="_blank">Visit Example</a>

🔍 Output:

Visit Example
