<track> - Text Tracks

🧠 What it does:

  • Adds timed text tracks to <video> and <audio> elements.
  • Commonly used to add subtitles, captions, descriptions, chapters, or metadata.
  • It is a self-closing (void) element, which means it has no closing tag.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To provide subtitles for video content.
  • To add closed captions for users who are deaf or when the sound is muted.
  • To enhance accessibility by providing a transcription or translation of dialogue and other relevant audio information.

💻 Syntax:

<video controls>
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <track src="subtitles_en.vtt" kind="subtitles" srclang="en" label="English">
</video>
