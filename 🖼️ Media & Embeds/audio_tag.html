<audio> - Audio Element

🧠 What it does:

  • Embeds sound content into a webpage.
  • It allows you to play audio files like MP3, OGG, or WAV directly in the browser.
  • The tag provides built-in controls for play, pause, and volume adjustment.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To add background music or sound effects to a page.
  • For embedding podcasts or recorded interviews.
  • When you want to provide a pronunciation guide for language learning apps.

💻 Syntax:

<audio controls>
    <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
    <source src="audio.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>
