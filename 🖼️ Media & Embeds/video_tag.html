<video> - Video Player

🧠 What it does:

  • Embeds a video player into a web page.
  • Used to embed video content in a document.
  • It can contain one or more ` ` tags to specify different video formats for browser compatibility.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To add video content, such as movie clips or video streams, directly to your webpage.
  • To create a video player with controls for play, pause, and volume.
  • When you want to provide different video formats to ensure the content is playable on a wide range of devices.

💻 Syntax:

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
    <source src="movie.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <source src="movie.ogg" type="video/ogg">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
