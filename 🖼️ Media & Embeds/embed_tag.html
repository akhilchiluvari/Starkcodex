<embed> - External Content

🧠 What it does:

  • It embeds external content or a plugin in a document.
  • It is a self-closing tag and does not require an end tag.
  • It can handle a wide range of content types, including audio files, video files, and PDFs.

🕰️ When to use:

  • To embed external resources that require a plugin or an external application to display.
  • When you need to embed content that is not natively supported by HTML, such as legacy Flash animations.
  • You can also use it to embed images, audio, or video, although it's often recommended to use the ``, `

💻 Syntax:

<embed src="path/to/your/file.pdf" type="application/pdf" width="500" height="400">
